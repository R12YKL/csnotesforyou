About CS Notes For You

CS Notes For You is a comprehensive resource designed to help students prepare for the Leaving Certificate Computer Science examination.

What We Offer

  • Past Papers: Access to years of Computer Science exam papers to practice with real questions.
  • Solutions & Marking Schemes: Study detailed solutions and marking schemes to understand the expected answers.
  • Study Notes: Organized topic notes with PowerPoint presentations to reinforce your learning.
  • Community Contributions: Share and learn from resources contributed by other students and teachers.

How to Use This Site

  1. Browse Past Papers to find exam questions to practice with.
  2. Check Solutions & Marking Schemes to understand how to approach questions.
  3. Review Study Notes for organized topic coverage with presentations.
  4. Contribute your own resources to help the community.
